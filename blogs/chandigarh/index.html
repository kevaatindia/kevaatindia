About Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University (CU) is a leading Indian institution offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Law, Architecture, Journalism, Animation, and other areas. Established in 2012, it has quickly grown to become one of the most preferred universities in North India.

The university boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class faculty, and strong industry connections. It has been ranked among the top universities in India by various ranking agencies and is known for its excellent placement records.

Admission Process

Admission to Chandigarh University is based on merit and performance in entrance examinations. The university conducts CUCET (Chandigarh University Common Entrance Test) for most programs.

Application Steps:

Online Application

Fill the application form available on the university website with required details and documents.

Entrance Examination

Appear for CUCET or submit valid scores of JEE, CAT, GATE, etc. as applicable.

Counseling Session

Attend the counseling session for program selection and document verification.

Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat in the selected program.
