About Chandigarh Group of Colleges

Established in 2001, Chandigarh Group of Colleges (CGC) has emerged as a prominent educational institution in North India, offering a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. With campuses in Landran and Jhanjeri, CGC is affiliated with I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University and is accredited with an 'A+' Grade by NAAC. The institution is renowned for its emphasis on academic excellence, industry integration, and holistic student development

Why Choose Chandigarh Group of Colleges?

Academic Excellence Top Rating

CGC has received NAAC 'A+' accreditation and is recognized for its quality education and state-of-the-art infrastructure

Industry Integration

The institution maintains strong ties with industry leaders, ensuring that students are industry-ready through practical training and placement opportunities

Global Opportunities

CGC has established partnerships with renowned international institutions, offering students opportunities for dual degrees and global exposure

Admission Process

CGC follows a transparent and merit-based admission process

Application Procedure:

Online Application

Complete the online application form with academic details, upload required documents, and pay the application fee.

Entrance Examination

Appear for relevant entrance exams such as JEE Main, CAT, or university-specific tests, depending on the program.

Counseling Session

Participate in the counseling process for program selection, document verification, and fee structure discussion.

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Confirm your admission by paying the fees and complete the enrollment formalities.
