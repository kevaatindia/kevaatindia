Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kevaat? ▼

Kevaat helps you talk to students who are already studying in the universities you’re interested in. Through a 30-minute video call, you can ask questions and get real, honest answers about university life, academics, and everything you want to know.

Why should I trust the information shared by the Student Mentors? ▼

Our Student Mentors are real students currently studying at the universities you're considering. They share their own experiences, not paid reviews, so you get honest, up-to-date insights to help you make the right university decision.

Can I speak to Student Mentors from multiple universities? ▼

Yes! If you're unsure which university to choose, you can talk to student mentors from multiple universities. Each session gives you a 30-minute one-on-one video call with a student mentor from a different university, helping you compare and make the best decision. You can book as many sessions as you need!

How do I become a Student Mentor?? ▼

If you’re already studying in a college or university and want to help others while earning some money, you can join as a student mentor. Just go to our 'Join as Mentor' section, fill out the form, and we’ll get back to you.

What if I’m not satisfied with the session? ▼

We want you to have a good experience. If you didn’t find the session helpful, just let us know within 24 hours, and we’ll try our best to fix the issue
